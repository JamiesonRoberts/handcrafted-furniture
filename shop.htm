About Us

We are a family run custom cabinetry business serving Canada and the United States since 2002. Learning first hand in my early years, I was mentored by both my grandfather and my uncle in the craft of cabinety. Through the years I've continued to perfect those skills and with an added interest in period pieces, have studied tradional carving necessary to enhance certain styles. Creating one of a kind pieces of furniture is both a challenge and priviledge. We are always looking forward to working with our clients to create that special piece whatever it might be.
